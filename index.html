 My name is James Zajkowski. I am a game programmer and developer from Wisconsin with some experience in all core aspects of game development, including management. I have recently graduated from UW-Whitewater in Spring 2024 with a Bachelor of Computer Science major and a Media Arts and Game Design (MAGD) minor.

I have made multiple solo and team projects over the course of several years, some of which predate my college education. In my time at Whitewater, I attended the university's Game Development Club consistently since the start of the 2021 school year. In my last year there, I took on the role of club Vice President before I graduated later that Spring.

I am most experienced with:
  • Developing in the Unity Engine (first consistent use began ~2018)
  • Programming in C++ / C# via Visual Studio (learned introductory C++ in late 2017, learned C# shortly after)
  • Completing deliverables under hard deadlines (~3 years worth of completed game jams as of May 2024)
  • Safely sharing project files among teams via Github (first use was a 6-man team in 2021)
I have also recently began experimenting with Godot, though projects there are still in the works.
